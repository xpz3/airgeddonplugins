Administration - Firmware Upgrade

A firmware upgrade is available. To get the best experience, ASUS strongly recommends updating your router to the latest version of the firmware.

Note:
  1. The latest version of the firmware includes all updates from the previous version.
  2. To download and update the firmware, you must enter the WPA/WPA2 password and press the UPDATE button to continue.

  3. Downloading and updating the firmware may take a few minutes. During the update, do not turn off the router or close the browser.
